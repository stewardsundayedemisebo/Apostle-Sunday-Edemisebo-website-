About The Book

"The Kingdom Matters is a Scripture-based revelation of God's eternal purpose through Christ and His Kingdom..."

In this powerful first volume, Apostle Steward Sunday Edemisebo unveils the transformative power of Kingdom living. Far beyond religious routine, Kingdom Matters reveals how every believer is called to be an Agent of Transformation — bringing Heaven’s culture, authority, and glory into every sphere of life.

Through deep biblical insight and prophetic revelation, this book will:

  • Realign your understanding of God’s eternal agenda
  • Equip you with Kingdom principles for impactful living
  • Challenge you to move from spectator to active participant in God’s move on earth
  • Stir a burning desire for authentic revival and societal transformation

Whether you are a new believer or a seasoned minister, Kingdom Matters will ignite fresh passion for the King and His Kingdom.
