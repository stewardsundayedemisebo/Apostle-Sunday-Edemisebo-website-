About Apostle Sunday Edemisebo

Apostle Sunday Edemisebo is an itinerant minister of the Gospel and the Founder of Revival College Ministries, a ministry committed to raising mature believers through discipleship, intercession, sound doctrine, and the ministry of the Word.

Having encountered the Lord Jesus Christ at an early age, he has remained devoted to the apostolic mandate of equipping the saints for spiritual maturity, Kingdom stewardship, and transformational influence through the revelation of Christ and the ministry of the Holy Spirit.

Apostolic Assignment

To raise Kingdom stewards who accurately represent Christ, advance His Kingdom, and influence their generation through the power of the Gospel and the ministry of the Holy Spirit.
